    Choreografin, Tanzlehrerin, Ballerina

    Gabriela Velasco,

    Leiterin von geförderten internationalen Projekten wie "Bicicleta em Movimento" und "Solidao à flor da Pele", ist seit ihrem 16. Lebensjahr eine brasilianische staatlich geförderte Ballerina und Choreografin aus São Paulo, Brasilien. Sie ist Mitgründerin der Tanzkompanie "Balé da Cidade de Taubate" (Leitung von Direktorin Alexandra Luppe).

    In New York hat sie die VKIBC Goldmedaille gewonnen. Gabriela hat erfolgreich mit renommierten Choreografen wie Victor Navarro Capell, Luis Fernando Bongiovanni, Henrique Rodovalho, Jaruam Rodrigues und Darion Rodrigues (Cirque de Soleil) zusammengearbeitet und internationale Anerkennung erlangt. In São Paulo hat sie eng mit Andrea Pivatto zusammengearbeitet.

    Sie präsentierte auch beim Internationalen Tanzsolo Stuttgart - Solo Choreo in Deutschland, unterstützt vom Produktionszentrum Tanz & Performance e.V. Zusätzlich ist sie eine erfahrene Tanzlehrerin. Neben ihrer künstlerischen Karriere hat Gabriela an verschiedenen sozialen Projekten in Brasilien, Spanien und Kuba teilgenommen und Tanzunterricht für Kinder und Senioren gegeben. Außerdem hat sie Ballett- und Contemporary-Dance an verschiedenen Tanzschulen unterrichtet. Sie hat auch in verschiedenen Ländern wie den USA und Frankreich gearbeitet.

    Ballet Classic

    In Gabriela Velasco's ballet classes, students embark on a journey into the timeless beauty and precision of classical ballet. Classical ballet is the foundation of every form of dance, and under Gabriela's expert guidance, students dive into the fascinating world of classical ballet. Through a structured curriculum, students learn fundamental techniques focusing on posture, alignment, and grace. Gabriela guides students through a comprehensive study of ballet vocabulary and musicality, fostering awareness and precision in their movements. With personalized attention and constructive feedback, students develop strength, flexibility, and poise while cultivating a deep appreciation for the rich tradition of ballet.

    Brasilien Rythmos

    Gabriela Velasco introduces students to the vibrant and dynamic world of Brazilian contemporary dance, emphasizing traditional rhythms and modern techniques. This class explores:

    Coco-Seco

    Students immerse themselves in the percussive and energetic movements of Coco-Seco, a traditional dance from Northeastern Brazil. Emphasizing footwork, rhythm, and group synchronization, students learn to connect with the music's pulsating beats, expressing joy and community through dance.

    Maracatu

    In the Maracatu segment, students delve into the rich cultural heritage of this Afro-Brazilian dance. They explore the powerful and majestic movements that represent the coronation of African kings and queens in Brazil. Through intricate footwork and dramatic choreography, students embody the spirit and history of Maracatu.

    Ciranda

    Ciranda offers a gentle and inclusive dance experience, where students join hands and move in a circular formation, symbolizing unity and harmony. The class focuses on simple, flowing movements that connect participants with the traditional rhythms and communal aspects of Ciranda, fostering a sense of togetherness.

    Maracatu Rural

    Students experience the vibrant and colorful expressions of Maracatu Rural, a folkloric dance celebrating agricultural traditions. Emphasizing large, expressive movements and elaborate costumes, the class brings to life the lively spirit of rural celebrations, encouraging students to explore their creativity and cultural heritage. Through these classes, Gabriela Velasco empowers students to embrace the rich diversity of Brazilian contemporary dance, combining traditional rhythms with modern dance techniques to create a unique and expressive dance experience.
