    Choreographer, Dance Instructor, Ballerina

    Gabriela Velasco,

    Leader of funded international projects like "Bicicleta em Movimento" and "Solidão à flor da Pele," Gabriela Velasco has been a Brazilian state-supported ballerina and choreographer from São Paulo since the age of 16. She co-founded the dance company "Balé da Cidade de Taubaté" (under the direction of Alexandra Luppe).

    In New York, she won the VKIBC Gold Medal. Gabriela has successfully collaborated with renowned choreographers such as Victor Navarro Capell, Luis Fernando Bongiovanni, Henrique Rodovalho, Jaruam Rodrigues, and Darion Rodrigues (Cirque du Soleil), gaining international recognition. In São Paulo, she worked closely with Andrea Pivatto.

    She also presented at the International Dance Solo Stuttgart - Solo Choreo in Germany, supported by the Production Center for Dance & Performance e.V. Additionally, she is an experienced dance instructor. Alongside her artistic career, Gabriela has participated in various social projects in Brazil, Spain, and Cuba, teaching dance to children and seniors. She has also taught ballet and contemporary dance at various dance schools and worked in countries like the USA and France.

    Dance Classes

    Ballet Classes, Contemporary Dance, Stage Dance, Choreographic Creation

    Classical Ballet

    In Gabriela Velasco's ballet classes, students embark on a journey into the timeless beauty and precision of classical ballet. Classical ballet is the foundation of every form of dance, and under Gabriela's expert guidance, students dive into the fascinating world of classical ballet. Through a structured curriculum, students learn fundamental techniques focusing on posture, alignment, and grace. Gabriela guides students through a comprehensive study of ballet vocabulary and musicality, fostering awareness and precision in their movements. With personalized attention and constructive feedback, students develop strength, flexibility, and poise while cultivating a deep appreciation for the rich tradition of ballet.

    Contemporary Dance

    Gabriela Velasco introduces students to the dynamic world of contemporary dance, blending various dance styles to express complex emotions and stories. The class focuses on exploring innovative movements, floor work, and improvisation. Students are encouraged to experiment with personal expression and creativity, developing their unique style within the contemporary dance framework. Gabriela provides individual feedback, helping dancers refine their technique, musicality, and emotional expression.

    Brazilian Rhythms

    Gabriela Velasco introduces students to the vibrant and dynamic world of Brazilian contemporary dance, emphasizing traditional rhythms and modern techniques. This class explores:

    Coco-Seco

    Students immerse themselves in the percussive and energetic movements of Coco-Seco, a traditional dance from Northeastern Brazil. Emphasizing footwork, rhythm, and group synchronization, students learn to connect with the music's pulsating beats, expressing joy and community through dance.

    Maracatu

    In the Maracatu segment, students delve into the rich cultural heritage of this Afro-Brazilian dance. They explore the powerful and majestic movements that represent the coronation of African kings and queens in Brazil. Through intricate footwork and dramatic choreography, students embody the spirit and history of Maracatu.

    Ciranda

    Ciranda offers a gentle and inclusive dance experience, where students join hands and move in a circular formation, symbolizing unity and harmony. The class focuses on simple, flowing movements that connect participants with the traditional rhythms and communal aspects of Ciranda, fostering a sense of togetherness.

    Maracatu Rural

    Students experience the vibrant and colorful expressions of Maracatu Rural, a folkloric dance celebrating agricultural traditions. Emphasizing large, expressive movements and elaborate costumes, the class brings to life the lively spirit of rural celebrations, encouraging students to explore their creativity and cultural heritage.

    Through these classes, Gabriela Velasco empowers students to embrace the rich diversity of Brazilian contemporary dance, combining traditional rhythms with modern dance techniques to create a unique and expressive dance experience.
